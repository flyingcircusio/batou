Support
=======

batou itself is released "as is".

A public `mailing list <https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/batou>`_ is
there for general discussion and help.

You can also report bugs to our `bugtracker
<http://projects.gocept.com/projects/batou>`_.


Commercial support
------------------

We will be happy to give you commercial support for batou: feature
implementation, bug fixing, consulting, or education.

If you need us, send an email to `mail@gocept.com
<mailto:mail@gocept.com?Subject=Support%20for%20batou>`_ to get an offer for
our services.
